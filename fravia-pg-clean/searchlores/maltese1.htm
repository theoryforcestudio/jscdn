Coupla useful ways to look for anonymizers, by Corto, March 2002

An useful way to look for anonymizers it´s too remember that normally they appear under the form of two joined urls. The appropriate keywords in google give b.e. the following:
    http://www.chatos.com/proxy/special/nph-cyberanon.cgi/http://fravia.2113.ch/phplab/showclientheader.htm
    http://2ch.nekoruri.jp/nph-gate.cgi/fravia.2113.ch/phplab/showclientheader.htm
    http://adways.net/cgi2/nph-proxy.cgi/m0422/5/vq0199/0/101216689019826/http/fravia.2113.ch/phplab/showclientheader.htm
though we have obtained results (and an interesting parameter like nph that we might have already know, reading anonyweb), the question is if besides those it is possible to spot other anonymizers or, pseudoanonymizers, that is scripts that were not created with any specific anonymizing objective, and if we can avoid mere redirectors or simple links; so,

1º The first chain that called my attention was (modified by my personal addenda of a checker):
do/session/new

    http://partner.andbook.com/do/session/new/vsid/4496/url/http://fravia.2113.ch/phplab/showclientheader.htm
    http://www9.hmv.co.uk/do/session/4554647/tid/1/url/http://fravia.2113.ch/phplab/showclientheader.htm

The next step is, logically, a search with inurl: do session url. This allows us to increase our options.

But we have problems; another result is:
    http://www.panpartnership.co.uk/do/session/862664/vsid/867825/cid/282161/cmpid/867708/url/http://www.linguaphone.com/france/index.cfm
that gives 404 if we only change the address
    http://www.panpartnership.co.uk/do/session/862664/vsid/867825/cid/282161/cmpid/867708/url/http://fravia.2113.ch/phplab/showclientheader.htm
we have to reverse something more by shortening (session/862664/vsid/867825/cid/282161/cmpid/867708/)
    http://www.panpartnership.co.uk/do/url/http://fravia.2113.ch/phplab/showclientheader.htm
and, on the other hand, we can increase our horizons by shortening the search keywords as well, and only ask for vsid that offers:
    http://www9.hmv.co.uk/do/session/new/vsid/3073

a bad result unless we correct it by adding /url/ and cleaning /session/new/vsid/3073):
    http://www9.hmv.com/do/url/http://www.multiproxy.org/env_check.htm

also useful with the do/url, is to seek within results of searches, b.e.
+site:www.panpartnership.co.uk
