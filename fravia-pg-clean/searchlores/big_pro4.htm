I'm sure you can imagine what for a richness of uncensored data you can fish out of personal pages... personal pages providers know this very well themselves, eh, that's the very reason they give out -oh so altruistically- their space and bandwidth in the first place... seekers should know that on the web there is a GALAXY of almost 1000 personal pages providers - probably many more, who knows? - most of them with ad hoc search engines that you can use, many of them located outside the petty Euroamerican territories... search engines that your own-made searchbots will be able to query, automatically, en bloc :-) Note that many commercial bastards are 'selling' these very infos I'm giving out for free...
I'm still working on this stuff: at the moment in the following lists there are few direct links and no direct search engines forms referrals yet. Until I'll have found the time to add them, or until you'll send them, just paste the Name as an URL address and you'll land there... and -come to think of it- actually seekers usually just needs a name to find their way around... HEY! Here we go... TIMBER! TIMBER! Look there! ~S~ flying downhills like ugly (and clever) searching vultures!
