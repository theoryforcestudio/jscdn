A webbit search form

This simple yet useful google/[webbit] form was offered to [searchlores] by Selim in March 2009

Various terms combinations are possible. You could for instance launch a boolean OR query through a | for instance texts about [judo | karate] or music from [donovan | cohen]
You can either use one of the extensions listed below, or input whatever extension format you are looking for
This form should bypass most (unfortunately not all) bogus sites that fake "index.of" directories and pollute the search engines in order to lure zombies into buying stuff that is already on the web for free
Seekers can of course abuse the javascript text included (see the source, Luke: CTRL+U if you are using [opera] as you should) in order to finetune this form or to create more specific -or more powerful- forms
