The HOSTS file contains names for machines on the TCP/IP network. your computer will trust his own information before external information (via a DNS request).

What gordon's file does is associating well known annoying servers to the adress 127.0.0.1, which is the loopback adapter, ie your own compter. and since odds are you don't run a web server on your machine, when netscape tries to connect there, it fails and you don't get to see yet another ad or porn site picture... you'll get a 'broken picture' icon instead..
