Introduction

The way to go when you don't know how to search, or when you want to search some private paths of the web... :-)
I wont comment on the following programs, note that, as usual on the web, the simple fact that you know the names of these applications will give you a 'competitive advantage' vis-à-vis less competent searchers, hehe :-)

Note also how easy it is -through these names- to find sites discussing their relative advantages, for instance bearshare napster lime wire...
or, even better:
http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&as_qdr=all&q=bearshare+napster+lime+wire+%2Badvantage*
or
http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&as_qdr=all&q=bearshare+napster+limewire+%2Bcomparison
as ~Veliti~ proposed, or also: +"better than Napster" OR +"better than bearshare"
This said, you should never forget that most 'file sharing' appz do actively "phone home" all your data and searches...    bearshare napster limewire +spyware
so make sure your equipped to deal with these minor annoyances.

A tool designed to distribute large files over slow networks. Drawbacks or maybe enhancements to some people is that you have to search the web to find shared files.
