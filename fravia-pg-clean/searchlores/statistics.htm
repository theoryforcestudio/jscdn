"There are three kinds of lies: lies, damned lies, and statistics" (Disraeli)

Simplify numbers and statistics, and they will become a tag clearer.
Bias in sampling require that you use your own sense of proportion everytime you are confronted with statistical data. Relate those "numbers" to yourself and your country, in order to value them correctly.
Also remember that data collections are prone to misbehave, because of human answers (typical and very frequent birthday when people are in a hurry filling some form: 11.11.1911, since 00/00/0000 will not be accepted).

There is a wealth of information on the web, as this statistical data searching section testifies. I have gathered here only a broad 'palette' of tools, readers that have found/will find better tools are kindly requested to deliver their own pointers.
