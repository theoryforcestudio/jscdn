The third laboratory: Tag der Kristalltürme

Welcome! The aim of this third lab is nothing less and nothing more than to try to shed some light about the terrible events of 11 September 2001!
This may sound like preposterous bragging, yet I have the outmost confidence in the richness of the web and in my fellow seeker's capacities.
You will be able to grab important data through appropriate "database entering", "news-perusing", "text-exegesis" and all the various stalking techniques.

As you will see, this assignement will test at the same time your searching, your combing, your stalking, your evaluating and your reality cracking skills.
