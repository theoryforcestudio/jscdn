Introduction 

I'm happy to present to my reader this small compilation of Italian search engines that has the added advantage (for 'subsidiarity' seekers) to point out the 'micro-local' regional area inside Italy itself. I'm convinced that this kind of microgranularity will be more and more important for serious searching & combing & stalking purposes.
