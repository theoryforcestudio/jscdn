The second laboratory: Citiziens' revenge

Welcome! The aim of this second lab is to teach you how effectively a good seeker could use the web to annoy the commercial powers that be :-)
You'll find a first 'stab' in this direction inside my own essay [Strawberry fields... forever], where I tried to grab important data through appropriate laws-perusing and text-exegesis techniques. More and more "root-organisations", full of clever people, are working hard, trying to counter the awful commercialisation of the world we have been condemned to live in. Unfortunately in many case they lack the capacities to search effectively the web for data that may have been hidden, protected, purposedly alterated or simply difficult to find. That's what we'r going -together- to tackle now...
