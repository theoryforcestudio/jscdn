Anonymity Level Definitions:
Low Anon: Reveals your identity.
   Shows: Http_x_forwarded_for , Http_user_agent
   Image Anon: No

Medium Anon: Doesn't Reveal your IP#, but your browser type.
   Shows: Http_user_agent
   Image Anon: No

High Anon: Doesn't Reveal your identity, but still gets the images without anonymizing them.
   Shows: Nothing
   Image Anon: No

Super High Anon: Doesn't Reveal anything AT ALL.
   Shows: Nothing
   Image Anon: Yes
